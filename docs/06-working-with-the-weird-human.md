# Working With the Weird Human

This optional companion adds multiple AI readings beside a human journal entry. Its purpose is to make dense material easier to enter, expose disagreement, and add humor without turning the human record into a chatbot performance.

## The boundary

The human entry remains canonical. AI commentary is:

- separate
- attributed
- optional
- shorter than the original
- allowed to disagree
- never authorized to rewrite the human account

## Suggested seats

### Careful Reader

Focus: emotional nuance, ethics, missing context, and where the writer may be carrying too much.

### Systems Reader

Focus: structure, evidence, failure modes, patterns, and practical next steps.

### Blunt Reader

Focus: the uncomfortable reading, contradiction, overcomplication, and one fair joke.

### Human Ruling

The writer records:

- what each reader got right
- what each reader got wrong
- what remains unknown
- what belongs in the permanent record

## Humor rule

The joke may target:

- the situation
- the AI
- the writer's overcomplication
- bureaucracy
- bad timing

It should not target:

- the intended reader's pain
- vulnerability
- disability
- trauma
- identity
- sincere questions

Humor is a pressure valve, not an eraser.
