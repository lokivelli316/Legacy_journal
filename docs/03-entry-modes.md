# Entry Modes

Choose the smallest mode that preserves what matters.

## Pulse — 30 to 90 seconds

Use when energy is low or presence is the entire purpose.

- Date
- One true sentence
- One point of connection
- Closing

## Breath — 3 to 5 minutes

Use when one moment, feeling, question, mistake, or small joy stayed with you.

- What stayed
- Why it mattered
- Who or what it connected to
- Optional question

## Conversation — 10 to 20 minutes

Use when the day has a story or lesson worth explaining.

- What happened
- What stayed with you
- What it may mean
- Something honest about your limitation or uncertainty
- A point the reader may challenge

## Record — as long as needed

Use for complex work, major reflection, repair, or discovery.

Suggested sections:

- Context
- What happened
- What changed
- What failed or remains open
- What was learned
- What matters to the future reader
- Evidence and uncertainties
- Next action
