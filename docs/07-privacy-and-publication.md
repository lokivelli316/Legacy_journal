# Privacy and Publication Checklist

## Before using AI

- Remove names and unique identifiers that are unnecessary to the task.
- Do not paste credentials, private legal documents, medical records, or another person's private messages.
- Decide whether the AI provider may retain submitted content.

## Before committing to Git

- Confirm the file belongs in the public repository.
- Search for names, handles, email addresses, phone numbers, exact locations, account numbers, and unique project names.
- Check image metadata and screenshots.
- Confirm the `private/` directory remains ignored.
- Review the staged diff before committing.

## Before publishing an adaptation

- Preserve the private original separately.
- Rewrite roles generically.
- Generalize dates and places.
- Remove unnecessary family, medical, legal, financial, and recovery detail.
- Strip DOCX/PDF author metadata and revision identifiers.
- Run an independent privacy review.
- Read the public version as though you were a stranger trying to identify the people involved.

## When in doubt

Do not publish yet. Uncertainty is a valid status.
