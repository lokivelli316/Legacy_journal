# Prompt: Grounded Affirmations

Read today's entry and the prior excerpts supplied below.

Create five affirmations grounded only in that evidence:

1. permission-based
2. evidence-based
3. accountability-preserving
4. biological-constraint aware
5. tiny-step for the next hour or day

Rules:

- No grandiose, absolute, or motivational-poster language.
- Do not say the writer is healed, unstoppable, fearless, perfect, or guaranteed to succeed.
- Do not deny pain, consequences, uncertainty, or previous harm.
- Prefer “I am allowed,” “I am learning,” “I have already shown,” and “For today, I can.”
- Name the evidence supporting each affirmation.
- Recommend the most believable option, not the strongest-sounding option.
