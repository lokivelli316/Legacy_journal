# Example — Hard Day

**Mode:** Conversation  
**Truth status:** Written today  
**Intended reader:** Trusted person

Today I was overloaded and nearly answered a small disagreement as though it were an emergency.

I paused before replying. The pause did not solve the disagreement, but it stopped the pressure from choosing the tone.

I do not know whether the other person understood my silence. I do know that waiting prevented me from adding damage.

Point to challenge: A pause can be responsible, but it can also become avoidance if I never return.
