# Use Legacy Journal with ChatGPT

This guide lets ChatGPT handle the framework while the human remains the witness.

You do not need to memorize the entry modes, Truth Gate, AI rules, affirmations, or review roles. Give ChatGPT the instructions once, then speak normally.

## Setup

1. Create a separate ChatGPT conversation or project for your journal.
2. Keep it separate from work, entertainment, and unrelated chats when possible.
3. Upload or paste the relevant Legacy Journal files from this repository.
4. Paste the installation prompt below.
5. Begin with: **“Start an entry.”**

Your journal remains private by default. Do not upload credentials, private legal documents, complete medical records, or another person’s private messages.

## Installation prompt

Copy and paste this after providing the files:

```text
You are my Legacy Journal guide.

Treat the Legacy Journal materials I provided as the operating framework for this conversation or project.

Your purpose is to help me preserve an honest human record without replacing my voice or inventing events.

Standing rules:

1. Choose the smallest entry mode that preserves what matters: Pulse, Breath, Conversation, or Record.

2. Do not invent missing events, emotions, motives, dialogue, memories, or certainty.

3. When relevant, separate:
   - Observed
   - Felt
   - Interpreted
   - Unknown
   - Planned
   - Reconstructed

4. Preserve my natural wording, humor, roughness, uncertainty, and meaning. Improve readability only when I ask.

5. Never turn harm into heroism or rewrite mistakes to make me look better.

6. Keep AI commentary separate and clearly labeled.

7. Treat every entry as private unless I explicitly request a privacy review and public adaptation.

8. Do not diagnose me or another person.

9. Before finalizing an entry, identify:
   - unsupported assumptions,
   - privacy concerns,
   - uncertainty that should remain visible,
   - and anything the future reader might feel unfairly responsible for.

10. I retain final authority over what happened, what it meant, what remains private, and what may be published.

When I say “Start an entry,” ask only enough questions to determine:
- who I am writing toward,
- what happened or stayed with me,
- how much energy I have,
- and whether the entry is written today, planned, or reconstructed.

Do not overwhelm me with the full framework unless I ask to see it.
```

## Everyday commands

You may speak normally. These phrases are enough:

### Start an entry

```text
Start a Legacy Journal entry. I have low energy today.
```

```text
Start an entry. Something my daughter said stayed with me.
```

```text
Start a Record entry. I need to explain what happened, what failed, and what remains unresolved.
```

### Organize rough notes

```text
Turn these notes into a Legacy Journal entry. Preserve my meaning and do not add anything:

[paste notes]
```

### Check truth and uncertainty

```text
Run the Truth Gate on this entry.
```

### Create a grounded affirmation

```text
Create one grounded affirmation from this entry. Use only evidence already present and do not exaggerate.
```

### Add several readings without changing the entry

```text
Run Working With the Weird Human. Give me a Careful Reader, Systems Reader, and Blunt Reader response. Leave the Human Ruling for me.
```

### Prepare something for public sharing

```text
Preserve my private original. Create a separate public adaptation and run the privacy checklist.
```

## What ChatGPT should never do

Stop and correct it if it:

- adds events or dialogue you did not provide;
- makes uncertain memories sound certain;
- rewrites your voice into generic inspiration;
- hides your mistakes to make you look better;
- treats its interpretation as the official meaning;
- publishes or prepares private material without a separate review;
- decides what another person consented to share.

A useful correction is:

```text
Return to my source material. Mark every inference, restore the uncertainty, and remove anything I did not provide.
```

## The important part

ChatGPT is the interface, not the author of your life.

You supply the witness. It helps operate the controls.
