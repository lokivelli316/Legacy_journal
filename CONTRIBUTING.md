# Contributing

Contributions are welcome when they preserve the project's central boundary:

> AI may assist the record. It may not silently become the witness.

## Good contributions

- New neutral entry skeletons
- Accessibility improvements
- Privacy-preserving export tools
- Better prompts for separating facts from interpretation
- Additional language translations
- Optional AI reviewer roles
- Tests or scripts that detect likely personal information

## Do not submit

- Real private journal entries
- Another person's story without clear permission
- Medical or mental-health claims presented as diagnosis or treatment
- Model-generated content falsely represented as human testimony
- Examples containing real addresses, phone numbers, account data, or identifying details

Use fictional or fully generalized examples.
